Rich in history rooted in its mission to advance education and innovation

Established to provide quality technical education, the institute has grown into a center of excellence recognized for its academic rigor, research contributions, and industry partnerships. Over the years, it has built traditions that emphasize discipline, integrity, and a commitment to societal progress. Celebrating annual science fairs, technology exhibitions, festivals and cultural events has become part of its identity, fostering both intellectual and community spirit. These traditions, combined with a legacy of producing skilled professionals and innovators, reflect the institute’s enduring role in shaping the future of science and technology.
