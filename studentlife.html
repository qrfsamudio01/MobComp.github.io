Life at
CIST

is vibrant, dynamic, and designed to nurture both academic excellence and personal growth. Students enjoy a balanced experience that combines rigorous learning with opportunities for recreation, creativity, and community building.

CIST believes in a healthy mind in a healthy body. The campus includes facilities for basketball, volleyball, badminton, and futsal, alongside fitness gyms and wellness programs. Regular intramural tournaments and sports festivals foster teamwork, leadership, and school spirit.

CIST offers a modern and tech-driven campus, featuring smart classrooms, collaborative learning hubs, innovation labs, and digital libraries. Beyond academics, students can explore maker spaces, and research incubators that encourage creativity and innovation.

From tech expos and innovation fairs to cultural nights and sports fests, student life at CIST is filled with memorable experiences that build lifelong friendships and professional networks.

Students can engage in research-focused societies, join experimental and innovation clubs, participate in scientific forums, and collaborate in problem-solving groups that bring theoretical knowledge into practical applications.
